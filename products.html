Telegram Notice


To facilitate efficient and prompt dissemination of important notice messages, we have established a Telegram notice channel that allows users to submit updates via HTTP application programming interface (API). This channel is readily accessible to anyone, and offers a convenient platform for sending notice messages.


How to use

  • Step 1: Create a Telegram account and download the Telegram application.
  • Step 2: Click the link to join the DantengNoticeBot channel.
  • Step 3: Send the /start command to the channel to obtain your unique notice URL and Token.
